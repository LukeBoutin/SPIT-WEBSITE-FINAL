ABOUT SPIT

SPIT IS THE S@#%T 

Spit is a multifaceted company that has developed in the emergence of skateboarding. Designed in 2001 Spit has shown the underbelly of skateboarding and skate culture. “If it’s polished we don’t want it”. Spit is here for a good time not a long time. Inspired by grunge culture and punk rock spit is in your face through and through.

Its owners Mikey Sasbah and Josef Strickem both are core skated from Manchester TN. They both originally grew up skating together oddly enough in aspen CO. they where both on vacation with parents and happened to meet. they skated and snowboarded together every day. They both found out they actually where from the same town so when they returned home their friendship began. (We know corny right). From then on the rest is history.
