SPITS NEWEST TEAM MEMBER

Written by Luke Boutin




SPIT hocks an entirely new kind of loogie this week as it welcomes its newest rider NOAH PD. Noah is a local rider out of SEWSICK Massachusetts. Noah got his start early on and has been a powerhouse ever since. Noah locals at MAC PARKS, Loon, and WAWA. Kids an animal what can we say? From his performance at chair 46 with an insane win for the teen category. Noah was throwing tricks like under flips, doubles, mad transfers and absolutely crushing the jump section. Noah ended the comp off as a real showman by dowsing himself in the pond at the end of the jam. With all this enthusiasm out members were noting but fired up to bring this kid online.

Noah is currently riding for Salmon, Bent metal, “FTP”, SHITHOUSEBOARDS, and now SPIT. His whole ass vibe just fits the madhouse and crazy aesthetic.
